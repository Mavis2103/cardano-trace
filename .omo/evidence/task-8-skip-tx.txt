Changes Made
------------

1. cache.py L1089: Removed `if processed_by_addr else None` fallback
   • Before: processed_by_addr=processed_by_addr if processed_by_addr else None
   • After:  processed_by_addr=processed_by_addr
   • Reason: Empty dict {} was being converted to None, losing the distinction
     between "no data" (None) and "empty dict" ({}). Now always returns dict.

2. cli.py L1026-1044: Removed target-only fallback
   • Before: cached_partial.processed_by_addr or {address: cached_partial.processed | cached_partial.failed}
   • After:  cached_partial.processed_by_addr or {}
   • Reason: The old fallback only covered the target address, meaning
     counterparty tx hashes at depth > 1 were never skipped even when cached.
     With B2's processed_by_addr correctly populated for ALL addresses,
     the fallback is now just {} (empty dict → no skipping).
